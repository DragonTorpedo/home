§ 04 — READING THE INSTRUMENT

Notes on using this

Why would I need more than one AI?

Because "write me an email" and "refactor this repo" aren't the same job, and no single model is tuned for both. Most people who settle on one tool are optimizing for familiarity, not fit.

My work answers and home answers would be different.

That's expected. The first question sets your context, and the closing notes call out where work and home life would actually pull you toward different tools.

Is this scored scientifically?

No — it's a structured way of thinking through the same twenty task categories in the reference chart above, weighted by how you actually spend your week. Treat the result as a strong starting point, not gospel.

What's with the theme button?

Day and night are the two intended readings. There's also a maintenance mode. You won't find it by reading the FAQ.
