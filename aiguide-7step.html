The Framework

1

Foundation

Identity Architecture
The Digital Clone

GPT-5.5 Instant
Gemini 3.1 Pro

Before any task, build your foundation. The AI must answer through your personal lens — not a generic one. This step produces three files that persist across every session you ever run.

📄
user.md
Your role, domain, and working principles. Tells the AI who you are professionally.
🧬
soul.md
Your voice, values, and communication style. Keeps output sounding like you — not like AI.
🎯
identity.md
Who the AI is to you. Define it as "Chief of Staff," "Research Partner," or whatever fits your workflow.
🎙️
The Interview Method
Unsure what to write? Ask GPT-5.5 to interview you about your values, then have it draft all three files from your answers.

Why GPT-5.5 or Gemini Pro

GPT-5.5 Instant's interview capability extracts your identity through dialogue. Alternatively, Gemini 3.1 Pro's "Personal Intelligence" setting persists these preferences across all connected Google apps automatically.

2

Input

Curated Scoping
The Curate Pillar

NotebookLM
Gemini 3.1 Pro

Garbage in, garbage out. The biggest mistake is dumping unvetted sources into an AI. Step 2 creates a clean, trusted knowledge base before a single line of execution begins.

📚
Vet Your Sources
Only upload links, PDFs, or videos from authors you trust. NotebookLM becomes your hallucination-free knowledge base.
📋
Planning Mode PRD
Use Gemini 3.1 Pro in Planning Mode to produce a detailed Product Requirements Document before any execution starts.
🔍
Blind Spot Scan
Prompt: "Based on these sources, what important subtopics are missing? Do not invent details." Closes gaps before they become errors.

Why NotebookLM + Gemini Pro

NotebookLM grounds every answer in your verified sources. Gemini Pro's Planning Mode then turns that clean knowledge into a structured PRD — a reusable blueprint for everything in steps 3–7.

3

Brainstorm

Psychological Modeling
Interactive Scaffolding

Gemini Canvas
GPT-5.5 Thinking

Move your raw ideas into an interactive workspace. The goal here is to make it structurally difficult for the AI to deviate from your logic and voice.

🖼️
Canvas Environment
Gemini's Canvas lets you highlight any section and give live directives: "be firmer here," "more persuasive," "cut this." Real-time sculpting, not re-prompting.
🧠
Board of Advisors
Tell the AI to embody a mastermind of people who've achieved your specific goal. Steve Jobs for design decisions. MrBeast for hooks. Specific personas beat generic instructions.
📐
PSL Framework
Ask GPT-5.5 Thinking to reformat your ideas into Point, Story, Lesson. Every output gets a clear takeaway — no more rambling.

Why Gemini Canvas + GPT-5.5 Thinking

Canvas gives you surgical control over tone and emphasis in real time. GPT-5.5 Thinking applies structured reasoning to reformat your brainstorm into airtight output frameworks — the two together turn messy thinking into clean execution-ready material.

4

Execution

Agentic Skills
Deep Execution

NotebookLM
Claude Opus 4.8

This is the premier 2026 workflow. Transition from manual prompting to automated pipelines. NotebookLM holds the facts. Claude executes the tasks — at massive scale, in parallel.

⚡
The Duo Strategy
NotebookLM as your hallucination-free knowledge base. Claude Skills as your execution engine. Never conflate the two roles.
🤖
Parallel Sub-Agents
Use Claude Code to spin up parallel agents via Dynamic Workflows. Each agent targets a different angle — logic, style, performance — simultaneously.
⏰
Scheduled Actions
Set background tasks to run research or audits on a schedule. Long-term goals run while you sleep.

Why Claude Opus 4.8

Opus 4.8's Dynamic Workflows via Claude Code can orchestrate hundreds of parallel sub-agents that plan, execute, and verify work simultaneously — no other model in the 2026 lineup matches this for complex engineering or data tasks.

5

Refinement

Stylistic Cloning
Logic Linking

Claude Fable 5
Opus 4.8 X-High

Refine the look, feel, and logic of every output until it sounds and reasons exactly like the best version of your work. This step is about precision — not production.

✍️
Style Analysis
Give Claude a piece of content you love and prompt: "Analyze the tone, cadence, and logic links. Create a Style Guide Artifact from this."
🔗
X-High Reasoning
Toggle X-High Reasoning on Claude Opus 4.8 to force deeper logical iteration and proactive self-correction before the final output is shown.
🌟
Adaptive Thinking
Claude Fable 5 features always-on adaptive thinking — the model determines its own reasoning depth. Use it when accuracy is non-negotiable.

Why Claude Fable 5 or Opus 4.8

Fable 5 is Anthropic's most capable model with always-on adaptive thinking. Opus 4.8 with X-High Reasoning forces the model into deeper logical iteration — use either when the cost of a wrong answer is high.

6

Finalization

Token Optimization
Hot-Swapping

Claude Haiku 4.5
Gemini Pro Fast

Don't burn Opus-level tokens on tasks that don't need them. The final mile is about cost-efficiency — swap to lighter models, preserve your reasoning budget for what matters.

🔄
Model Hot-Swap
Once strategic decisions are locked, switch to Haiku 4.5 or Gemini Fast Mode for formatting, translation, and copyediting. Near-frontier output at a fraction of the cost.
🚫
.geminiignore
Create an ignore file to tell the model which assets to skip — images, logs, heavy data. Saves significant context window space on large projects.
💾
Convert to Source
Once your output is perfect, convert that note into a source in NotebookLM. That "perfect" logic persists for all future tasks in the project.

Why Haiku 4.5 or Gemini Pro Fast

Both deliver near-frontier performance at significantly lower token cost. Reserving your high-reasoning quota for Steps 4–5 means you can run a complete 7-step workflow without hitting limits.

7

Compound Growth

Nightly Compounding
The 0.01% Loop

Claude Scheduled

The real edge isn't the prompt — it's the system that runs while you sleep. Step 7 turns your AI setup into a compounding machine that gets smarter every single day without your input.

🌙
Nightly Review
Use Claude's scheduled loops to read everything added to your knowledge base that day. Runs automatically at a set time.
🔗
Orphan Note Detection
The loop identifies "orphan notes" — mentions of people or projects that don't have their own files yet — and flags them for follow-up.
📊
Consolidation
Duplicates are merged. Maps of Content are updated. You wake up to a smarter, more connected system every morning — automatically.

Why Claude Scheduled Tasks

Claude's native scheduling hooks let you run cron-style tasks without external infrastructure. Program it once. The system compounds daily. This is the difference between using AI and operating AI.
